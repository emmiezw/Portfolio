WHAT'S FOODE

A communication platform that connects restaurant owners and customers with honest and private feedbacks

Food-e is an entrepreneur project based in University of Michigan. It is an iOS & Android app that bridges restaurant owners and customers with high-quality, depressurized, and private reviews. Customers can provide anonymous honest feedbacks to obtain rewards while restaurant owners can condense insights to help improve and maintain customer loyalty.


MY ROLES & AGILE DEVELOPMENT

UX roles in Agile Development

This 3-months project came to our 6-person team as an abstract idea in our Master Capstone Course (Agile Development Track). Collectively, we worked on initial user research, concept testing, UX Design, usability testing, and software development.

After the course ends, I worked on an individual iteration of current end-to-end user experience based on our testing results.

MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT

User research and initial concept testing

Two major stackholders

Restaurant Owners Wants

  • Quality Review: I read through 300+ comments on the AppStore, and collected then into 73 affinity notes. This helps me identify user needs.
  • Actionable Insights: I ran 2 sessions of in-person in-gym usability testing sessions including: goal setting, workout tracking and progress reviewing. This helps me identify usability problems of the live app.
  • Customer Retention: I tried following a plan for a week (4 training days). This helps me gain empathy for users.

Customers Wants

  • PERKS of course!: I sent out surveys about why people stick to/or couldn't stick to their training plan, and got 31 responses. This helps me validate user needs and identify insights for designing behavior change.
