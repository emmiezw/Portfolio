Trimble - Dashboard Redesign

SaaS Enterprise Product Data Visualization User Flow

Trimble Unity Remote Monitoring software platform offers a suite of applications and hardwares to support smart water management. Customers are seeking for an effective dashboard to identify malfunction of hardwares or abnormality in water data to take actions quickly on a daily basis.

This design focused on providing actionable insights for customers. It answers the following questions:

1. Data Visualization: What are the useful information to users and how we present these information?
2. Dashboard Navigation/User Flow Design: How might we allow users to take actions quickly based on the insights they draw from the dashboard?

Project Length

2 months (2/2020-3/2020)
