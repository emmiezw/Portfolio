INTRODUCTION

Our project is a virtual reality application for children at C.S. Mott Children’s Hospital to experience University of Michigan sporting events.

In order to reduce isolation and improve the quality of life for patients, we scoped, designed, tested, iterated, and implemented Gameday VR to create a safe and accessible 360° sport video viewing experience.
